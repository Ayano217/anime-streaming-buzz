## Light Novel 'Shin no Seijo de Aru Watashi wa Tsuihou saremashita. Dakara Kono Kuni wa Mou Owari desu' Gets TV Anime in 2027

An official website opened for a television anime adaptation of Shikiso Utsuzawa&#039;s Shin no Seijo de Aru Watashi wa Tsuihou saremashita. Dakara Kono Kuni wa Mou Owari desu (Because I, the True Saint, Was Banished, That Country Is Done For!) light novel on Thursday, revealing the main cast, staff, and a teaser visual (pictured). The anime series will premiere in 2027. Voice actors Masumi Tazawa (Rokujouma no Shinryakusha!?) and Taku Yashiro (Domestic na Kanojo) are starring as Eliane and Nige...
