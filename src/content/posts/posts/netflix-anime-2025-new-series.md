## Netflix Goes All In on Anime

Netflix has revealed its ambitious 2025 anime slate, featuring 15 new titles ranging from original productions to highly anticipated adaptations.

### Original Series

**New Netflix original anime:**

1. **Terminator Zero** Season 2 — The AI apocalypse continues
2. **Pluto** continuation — Naoki Urasawa's masterpiece
3. **Ghost in the Shell: New Project** — Cyberpunk classic returns
4. **Kaiju No. 8** Season 2 — Monster hunting continues

### Adaptations

**Manga and game adaptations:**

5. **Dandadan** (continuing from Crunchyroll co-production)
6. **Sakamoto Days** (co-production)
7. **Devil May Cry** anime — Game adaptation
8. **Dragon Age: Absolution** Season 2

### Movies

**Anime films coming to Netflix:**

9. **Bubble 2** — Follow-up to the 2022 film
10. **New Ghibli Partnership Film** — Details TBA
11. **Cyberpunk: Edgerunners Movie** — Expanded universe

### Exclusive Partnerships

Netflix has signed deals with:

- **Studio MAPPA** for 3 exclusive titles
- **Wit Studio** for 2 new projects
- **Science SARU** for experimental anime shorts
- **BONES** for a mystery project

### Our Analysis

Netflix's 2025 anime strategy shows they've learned from past mistakes. Instead of quantity over quality, they're focusing on:

- Established IP adaptations
- Sequel seasons of hits
- Strategic studio partnerships
- Global simultaneous releases

### How This Affects You

For anime fans, this means more options than ever. The competition between Netflix, Crunchyroll, and other platforms is driving higher quality productions.

**Our pick to watch:** Ghost in the Shell: New Project has the potential to be the anime event of 2025.
