## Web Manga 'Real mo Tama ni wa Uso wo Tsuku' Gets TV Anime in 2027

Manga author Niichi announced on their official X account on Sunday that their Real mo Tama ni wa Uso wo Tsuku (Sometimes Even Reality Is a Lie!) web manga is receiving a television anime adaptation. Niichi drew an illustration to commemorate the announcement (pictured). The anime series will premiere in 2027.
