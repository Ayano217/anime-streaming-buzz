## The Biggest Anime Season of 2025

Winter 2025 is shaping up to be one of the most stacked anime seasons in recent memory. Crunchyroll has secured an impressive lineup of both returning favorites and exciting new series.

### Top Returning Series

**Must-watch sequels this season:**

1. **Solo Leveling Season 2** — The manhwa adaptation continues
2. **Blue Lock Season 2** — The soccer death game intensifies
3. **Sakamoto Days** — The retired hitman returns
4. **Dandadan** — Supernatural comedy continues
5. **Dr. Stone: Science Future** — The final season

### New Series to Watch

**Fresh anime debuting this winter:**

1. **Lazarus** — Original sci-fi from Studio MAPPA
2. **The Apothecary Diaries Season 2** — Historical mystery
3. **Toilet-Bound Hanako-kun Season 2** — Supernatural school drama
4. **UniteUp! Season 2** — Idol music series
