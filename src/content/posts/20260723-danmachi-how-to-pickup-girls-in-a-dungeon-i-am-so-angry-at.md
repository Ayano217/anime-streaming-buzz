## Danmachi (How to pickup girls in a dungeon) I am so angry at myself for putting this off for so long.

How to pickup girls in a dungeon is the absolute worst possible name they could have picked for this unique anime. I am angry at myself for overlooking it for so long just because I thought it was gonna be a stupid harem comedy. I am just finishing up the first season and absolutely loving it so far, I even picked up the first LN to read during my commute on train. I know people say the LN is better, and it is cool and fleshes out the world a lot more, but the anime is nothing to be scoffed at. &#32; submitted by &#32; /u/Inquisitor_Glokta_ [link] &#32; [comments]
