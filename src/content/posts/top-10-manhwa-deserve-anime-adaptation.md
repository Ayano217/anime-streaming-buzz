## Manhwa That Need Anime Adaptations

The success of Solo Leveling and Tower of God has proven that manhwa can make incredible anime. Here are 10 more series that deserve the spotlight.

### 10. Nano Machine

**Genre:** Action, Martial Arts, Sci-Fi

A martial arts story with a sci-fi twist. The protagonist receives nanotechnology from the future, giving him an edge in a ruthless martial arts world.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Incredible fight choreography potential and a unique premise.

### 9. Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

**Genre:** Action, Fantasy, Isekai

Already confirmed for anime adaptation. A man who read a web novel finds himself living inside it when the story becomes reality.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Complex narrative layers and massive fan base.

### 8. Eleceed

**Genre:** Action, Comedy, Supernatural

A cat-loving boy discovers he has superpowers in a world of hidden ability users.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Perfect balance of humor and action.

### 7. The Beginning After the End

**Genre:** Fantasy, Isekai, Action

A king reincarnates in a magical world and must grow strong again while facing threats from his past life.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Rich world-building and character development.

### 6. Windbreaker (manhwa)

**Genre:** Action, Drama

Street racing on bicycles with stunning art and emotional storytelling.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Visually dynamic action sequences.

### 5. Leveling with the Gods

**Genre:** Action, Fantasy

A warrior travels back in time to prevent the apocalypse by climbing the Tower.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Similar appeal to Solo Leveling with unique twists.

### 4. Return of the Mount Hua Sect

**Genre:** Martial Arts, Comedy, Action

A legendary swordsman reincarnates 100 years later and must rebuild his destroyed sect.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Hilarious protagonist with epic sword fights.

### 3. Mercenary Enrollment

**Genre:** Action, School Life

A child soldier returns to normal life and enrolls in high school, but trouble follows.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Satisfying action with emotional family themes.

### 2. Teenage Mercenary

**Genre:** Action, Drama

Similar to Mercenary Enrollment but with darker themes and more intense action.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Gritty, realistic action style.

### 1. Trash of the Count's Family

**Genre:** Fantasy, Comedy, Action

A man reincarnates as a minor villain in a fantasy novel and tries to live a quiet life — but keeps accidentally becoming a hero.

**Why it deserves an anime:** Unique anti-hero protagonist and clever storytelling.
