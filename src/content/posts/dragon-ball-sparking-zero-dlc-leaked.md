## New Fighters Incoming

Data miners have uncovered evidence of Dragon Ball: Sparking Zero's second DLC pack, and the findings have the community buzzing with excitement.

### Leaked Characters

**Reportedly coming in DLC 2:**

1. **Beast Gohan** — Final form from Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero
2. **Orange Piccolo** — Giant form transformation
3. **Gas** — Granolah arc antagonist
4. **Granolah** — The last Cerealian
5. **Black Frieza** — The ultimate Frieza form

### New Stages

Along with characters, two new battle stages were found:

- **Planet Cereal** — From the Granolah arc
- **Red Ribbon Base** — From Super Hero movie

### Gameplay Additions

The data mine also suggests:

- New dramatic finishes for existing characters
- Updated combo routes for base roster
- Quality of life improvements
- New online tournament mode

### Community Reaction

The Dragon Ball gaming community is split on the selections:

**Positive reactions:**

**Concerns:**

Based on previous DLC timing, DLC Pack 2 is expected to release in **March 2025**, approximately 3 months after DLC Pack 1.
