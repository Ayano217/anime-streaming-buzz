## The Curse Continues

In a surprise announcement during Jump Festa 2025, MAPPA officially confirmed that Jujutsu Kaisen Season 3 is in production. The announcement came with a teaser visual and several production details.

### What We Know

**Confirmed details:**

- Production is currently underway at MAPPA
- The season will adapt the Culling Game arc
- Expected premiere window is Fall 2025
- Key staff members are returning

### Production Changes

While MAPPA remains the studio, there are some notable changes:

1. New assistant director joining the team
2. Updated animation pipeline for complex fight scenes
3. Collaboration with freelance animators from across the industry
4. Enhanced CGI integration for large-scale battles

### The Culling Game Arc

For manga readers, the Culling Game arc represents one of the most ambitious story arcs in Jujutsu Kaisen. Key elements include:

- Multiple simultaneous battle colonies
- New cursed technique users
- Major character development for Megumi
- Game-changing revelations about the jujutsu world

### Fan Reaction

The announcement trended worldwide on social media within minutes:

- Over 2 million tweets in the first hour
- Crunchyroll's announcement video hit 5 million views
- Fan art flooded every platform

### Our Analysis

Season 3 has the potential to be the best season yet, but the Culling Game arc is also the most complex to adapt. The key will be pacing — rushing through the material could hurt the story.
