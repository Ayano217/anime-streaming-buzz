## Manga 'Hana wa Saku, Shura no Gotoku' Concludes Serialization

The official X (formerly Twitter) account for illustrator Musshu announced on Friday that Ayano Takeda&#039;s Hana wa Saku, Shura no Gotoku (Flower and Asura) manga will end in the August issue of Ultra Jump magazine. Takeda (Hibike! Euphonium) and Musshu began serializing the school drama manga in Ultra Jump magazine in June 2021. Shueisha published the tenth volume on March 18.
